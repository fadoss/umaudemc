# Bibliography

The most relevant articles regarding `umaudemc` are the following:

* *Strategies, model checking and branching-time properties in Maude* {cite:p}`smcJournal-btime` ([arXiv](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2401.07680), [BibTeX](https://dblp.org/rec/journals/jlap/RubioMPV21.html?view=bibtex)) for branching-time model checking and the architecture of the tool.
* *Model checking of strategy-controlled systems in rewriting logic* {cite:p}`smcjournal` ([arXiv](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2401.07680), [BibTeX](https://dblp.org/rec/journals/ase/RubioMPV22.html?view=bibtex)) for LTL model checking of strategy-controlled models.
* *QMaude: quantitative specification and verification in rewriting logic* {cite:p}`qmaude` ([archived](https://hdl.handle.net/20.500.14352/101033), [BibTeX](https://dblp.org/rec/conf/fm/RubioMPV23.html?view=bibtex)) for the quantitative extensions.
* *PMaude revisited through probabilistic strategies* {cite:p}`pmaude-strat` ([archived](https://hdl.handle.net/20.500.14352/132923), [BibTeX](https://dblp.org/rec/conf/birthday/Rubio0M26.html?view=bibtex)) as an illustrative example of probability assignment methods.
